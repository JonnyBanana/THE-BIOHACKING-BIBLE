Use at 1:1000 in liq media. 2ml/L in plates. ===DNA Miniprep=== # Soln I for miniprep: 2.3 ml 40% glucose, 2.5 ml 1M Tris 8, 2 ml 0.5M EDTA. To 100 ml with good H20. Store at RT # Soln II for miniprep: equal parts 2% SDS (2g/100 ml H20): 0.4M NaOH (1.6g/100 ml H20). Store components at RT. Mix just enough just before using. # Soln III for miniprep: 29.4 g KAc dissolved in 60 ml H20. Add 11.5 ml glacial acetic acid. Bring to 100 ml final volume. Store at RT. ===Agarose Gel=== # DNA gel: 1% agarose gel in 1X TAE, 1 g agarose, 100mL 1X TAE, 2 ul EtBr (wear nitrile gloves when handling EtBr!) # Loading dye for agarose gel: 250 ul 1% XC (xylene cyanol), 750 ul 40% glycerol, 10 ul RNase.
